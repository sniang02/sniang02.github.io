I am a third-year PhD student at the [Laboratoire J.A. Dieudonné](https://math.univ-cotedazur.fr/),
[Université Nice Côte d'Azur](https://univ-cotedazur.eu/), under the supervision of
[Prof. Charles Bouveyron](https://math.univ-cotedazur.fr/~cbouveyr/),
[Dr. Marco Corneli](https://math.univ-cotedazur.fr/~mcorneli/),
and [Prof. Pierre Latouche](https://lmbp.uca.fr/~latouche/).

My PhD research focuses on **deep generative models for the joint analysis of networks and continuous data**.
More broadly, my research interests lie at the intersection of *probabilistic modelling*, *statistical learning*,
and *machine learning*, with a particular emphasis on:

- **Graph-structured data** and network analysis
- **Deep generative models** (variational autoencoders, latent variable models)
- **Probabilistic inference** and variational methods
- **Applied statistics** and high-dimensional data

Feel free to reach out by email for any question related to my research.
